About the Boxes

DGFamily Boxes are digital collectibles (NFTs) that also serve as tiered membership to the exclusive Dolce&Gabbana universe. DGFamily members receive exclusive digital, physical, and experiential privileges as Dolce&Gabbana pushes the boundaries of luxury and culture in the metaverse. DGFamily boxes are based on the classic Italian Carretto style, and crafted in three increasingly rarer tiers.

The Black Box

The Black Box is bold and mysterious. Holding it grants access to the first level of the DGFamily: exclusive digital/physical drops of Dolce&Gabbana apparel, a series of digital/metaverse events, and special bonuses along the way, including a special Dolce&Gabbana swag set.

The Gold Box

The Gold Box is sleek, shiny, and more exclusive than the Black Box. Holding it grants all of the privileges of the Black Box, and more: access to more rare Dolce&Gabbana drops and invites to various IRL events. Plus a premium Dolce&Gabbana swag set with exclusive kicks.

The Platinum Box

By special access only. The Platinum Box is the pinnacle of the DGFamily community, and the most exclusive. It grants all of the privileges of the Gold Box, plus more: access to the rarest drops and most special IRL experiences, including an intimate Dolce&Gabbana offsite.

FAQs

Each Box is a digital collectable that also serves as membership to the DGFamily. Members will join Dolce&Gabbana on their journey to reshape fashion in the physical universe and the metaverse. Each Box unlocks exclusive digital, physical, and experiential privileges for its owner based on the tier of the Box.

DGFamily boxes come in three different tiers. In order of increasing rarity they are: Black, Gold, and Platinum. Each tier is granted the benefits of its tier as well as the tiers below. For example, the Platinum box unlocks the benefits of all tiers while the Gold box holders have access to the privileges of the Gold and Black tiers. Each Box tier grants access to a series of exclusive drops (both digital and physical) and experiences (digital and/or physical depending on the tier).

This project utilizes a minting whitelist to allow for a smoother minting process. The whitelist will consist of a collection of wallet addresses selected via raffle or special selection. Addresses on the whitelist will be authorized to mint the collection ahead of the public mint. Submit your details to Premint for a chance at a spot and join our Discord for more opportunities.

Boxes are eligible to claim or purchase certain physical, digital, and experiential benefits as they are created by Dolce&Gabbana and UNXD. Claiming will happen by minting the item on UNXD. Physical redemption will take place via a valid registered account on UNXD and require owners to pay for shipping and submit a valid mailing address. Physical event access will require owners to RSVP by the allotted date with personal details for the guestlist.

To be revealed soon via UNXD and Dolce&Gabbana on Twitter and Discord. Stay tuned.

Each Box comes with an exclusive swag set based on tier (gas/shipping excluded). Future drops may have a cost to mint and/or deliver the item. DGFamily holders will also be eligible for free airdrops from future drops and collaborations from time to time.

Each Box will first be minted on Ethereum Layer 1 for provenance before being transferred to owners’ wallets on UNXD’s platform, which is built on Polygon’s Layer 2 solution to benefit from lower fees and energy use.
