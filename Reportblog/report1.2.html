How to create an account

A step by step guide to creating an account on UNXD

3 articles in this collection

Written by UNXD

To bid for items or otherwise use UNXD, you will need to create a user account. Creating a user account includes submitting an email/password pair, verifying your email address, and adding a crypto wallet address.

Starting the account process

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the right-hand side of the navbar at the top of the screen.
  2. Enter you preferred personal email address, username, and password. Enter this password a second time to confirm you know the password. Be aware that the password must be at least 8 characters, contain at least 1 capital letter and digit.
  3. Press "Create Account"

Verifying your email address

  1. An email will be sent to the address you provided. If you don't receive an email within 5 minutes, search your mail client for "UNXD Verify" for the email. If you still can't find it, please check your spam folder or other tabs in your mailbox.
  2. When you open the email, click the "Verify" link to be returned to UNXD to continue the signup process by logging into your account

Adding a wallet address

UNXD is on the Polygon Network and supports Metamask. While you can skip this step, you will need to have a wallet attached to buy, sell, and collect NFTs.

Adding your Metamask wallet

  1. To add your Metamask wallet address, ensure you have your Metamask browser plugin set to the wallet id you would like to use with UNXD. If you don't have a metamask wallet, visit https://metamask.io/ to get a wallet and learn how to use it.
  2. Visit unxd.com/dashboard
  3. Click the Metamask button and agree to connect in the pop-up from Metamask to connect to the site.

After adding a wallet address, you will be able to fully use the site and bid on items. If you chose to skip this step, you can always connect a wallet later by returning to the wallet page, but won't be able to bid.
