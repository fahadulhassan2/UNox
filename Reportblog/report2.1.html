Balances and Preparing to Bid

How to load a spending balance and bid on UNXD

3 articles in this collection

Written by UNXD

UNXD is a curated NFT auction platform built on Polygon Network and denominated in ETH. Before you start, it's important to understand how the auction platform operates. Here are a few key points to keep in mind:

  • UNXD is built on Polygon, so you will need need wETH on Polygon Network.
  • All gas fees are handled by UNXD via Biconomy.
  • Biconomy enables use of the Platform while on Ethereum Mainnet.
  • Funds on UNXD move between Wallet Balance, Spending Balance, and Reserve Balance on a smart contract. This enables us to more easily support multi-sig, credit bidders, and secret bids on the platform.

Balances on UNXD

Funds on UNXD move between three balances: Wallet, Spending, and Reserve (Locked in Bids). These balances enable the platform to more easily handle multi-sigs, credit bids, and to prevent last minute bid sniping.

Bidding funds must be moved from your Wallet Balance (wrapped ETH on Polygon) to your Spending Balance, where they are locked up in a Smart Contract (0x3CEd67056f8F655eb80677508B0ceDc29926d95e) on Polygon Network.

Preparing to Bid

  1. Get a MetaMask

    A MetaMask wallet is required for bidding on UNXD. If you don't have a MetaMask, you can download it at https://metamask.io/

  2. Get wETH on Polygon Network

    To get ETH on Polygon Network, you need to use a Polygon Network bridge. Polygon has a bridge available at https://wallet.polygon.technology/bridge. Follow their directions to move the amount of ETH you would like to bid with from L1 to the Polygon Network.

  3. Create an account on UNXD

    Go to Sign Up on UNXD.com and create an account using email, username, and password. Then verify your email.

  4. Connect your MetaMask to your account

    Visit your account's dashboard, then connect your MetaMask account loaded with wETH to your account. While your wETH must be on Polygon Main, your RPC can remain on Ethereum Mainnet while you use the application.

  5. Transfer wETH from your wallet to your spending balance

    To bid on UNXD, you must have wETH in your spending balance. This balance is held in a smart contract and ready to be used for bidding. This balance can be recalled to the owner at any time by signing a transaction.

  6. First, visit the user dashboard. To transfer to your Spending Balance, click on "Transfer to Spending" below the "Wallet Balance" panel.

    Enter the amount you would like to have in your Spending Balance as your bidding budget. You can always add more to this later. After you have the amount you would like to transfer, click "Approve".

    After a few moments, the wETH will be approved for spending and you can proceed by pressing "Transfer". After some moments, the blockchain will confirm the transaction and your wETH will show up in your Spending Balance.
