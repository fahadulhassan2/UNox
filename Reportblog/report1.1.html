What is UNXD?

What is UNXD and what makes it special

3 articles in this collection

Written by UNXD

UNXD is a curated platform for premium digital art, fashion, luxury, and culture on the blockchain. We work with leading brands and creators to bring unique works and experiences to collectors via digital ownership.

On UNXD, collectors can use cryptocurrencies to purchase NFTs that represent ownership for digital goods as well as physical items, access to events and privileges, and unlocking enhanced digital content.

What Makes UNXD Different?

UNXD provides a curated experience focused on bringing high quality authentic digital goods to collectors. This focus comes from a team with a decade of experience in creation and luxury from running Vogue Arabia and WIRED Middle East. Vogue Arabia is one of the most widely seen editions of vogue, having reached over 300 Million people and been featured across media including The Tonight Show.

Additionally, UNXD mints NFTs capable of capturing advanced mechanisms tracking redemption for physical goods, RSVPs for events, and unlocking additional files. This ensures secondary buyers can clearly know what additional features remain with the NFT they are purchasing.
