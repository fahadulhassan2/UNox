Collezione Genesi

Collezione Genesi was first revealed from 28th-31st August 2021 at the Dolce&Gabbana couture event in the world’s most unique city: Venice. This 9-piece, one-of-a-kind collection was personally designed by Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana exclusively for UNXD. It features entirely hand-made, museum-grade items across Alta Moda (women), Alta Sartoria (men), and Alta Gioielleria (high jewelry). In a historic moment for the NFT ecosystem, Collezione Genesi is the first luxury NFT collection that involves both digital and physical works, truly bridging the physical and the metaphysical.

Alta Sartoria
